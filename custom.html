Summer Camps

Spring & Summer

IMG Academy Spring and Summer Camps provide young people with the opportunity to participate in outstanding academic, athletic, and artistic programs. These sessions are taught by qualified teachers who are experts in their respective fields, guaranteeing that your child will be both supported and challenged based on their level of skill and experience.

IMG Camps

IMG Summer Camps provide boys and girls with the ideal chance to have fun, make lasting memories, and receive the high-quality instruction they deserve, whether they're interested in exploring something new or improving their existing skill set.

A variety of in-person and online programmes are offered for the spring and summer of 2023.

Each session will:
  • Your child will be instructed by certified teachers who are experts in their respective fields
  • They will receive personalized instruction and assessment of their session-specific skills
  • They will be encouraged to engage in positive and enjoyable social interactions with their peers.
